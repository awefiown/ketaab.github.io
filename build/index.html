    How it works

    It all starts with a plan.

    • Step 1 — Establish contract agreement.

      We will go over with you what exactly it is that we can and cannot do. We will take a look at your historical data and discuss with you its relevance for our purposes in training your brand new neural network.

    • Step 2 — Begin feature engineering.

      Our highly specialised experiences have informed our data mining capabilities. In collaboration, we use our own knowledge to extract features from your data. Features are *attributes* or *labels*, or *tools to solve problems* that we then use to improve machine learning algorithms. We sit with you and brainstorm and test features, determine their relevance, decide on new features to create, perform quality checks of these features on various models, improve their operation, and go back through the steps of brainstorming and creating more features until a solution for your business needs is found.

    • Step 3 — Train your model with data.

      Here begins the crucial process of building a model. The natural first step is gathering data. The quality and quantity of the data you put in directly determines the quality and quantity of the output. Computer science terms this concept 'garbage in, garbage out' (GIGO). After data is gathered, we sanitize and prepare it it for machine learning training purposes. Using our models, we teach the neural network's to perform a task, while also training it on the back end to understand how accurately it is performing. Finally, we tune the parameters around the training and refine the model's ability to produce a desired outcome or solution.

    • Step 4 — Deploy new model.

      Whether your needs center on the filling out dense paperwork or the prediction of industry and business trends over time, your freshly built and trained predictive, descriptive, or decision model is capable of delivering highly accurate and efficient results on a reliable basis.
